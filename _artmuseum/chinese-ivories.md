  These intricately carved Chinese ivories showcase the extraordinary skill and artistry of their makers. Each piece tells a story of cultural exchange, luxury, and the global movement of ivory as both material and art. Their presence in the art museum invites us to reflect on the beauty, complexity, and ethical questions surrounding ivory objects from across the world.
