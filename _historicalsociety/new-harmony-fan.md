  <strong>Description:</strong> <br>
  This 19th century ivory fan is a testament to both artistic skill and the complex global networks that brought such objects to small-town America. While its style and beauty hint at metropolitan inspirations, this piece may have acccompanied New Harmony’s intrepid frontier residents during social gatherings. Despite its historical lustre, its material origin invites us to consider the environmental and ethical dimensions of its journey from elephant to artifact. What stories can this fan tell? Through it, we glimpse the intersection of local lives and global histories.
